Sour Cream Chicken Enchiladas Recipe

We have the easiest Sour Cream Chicken Enchiladas Recipe packed with chicken, cheese and an amazing sour cream sauce. These are the best Sour Cream Chicken Enchiladas and they get even better the next day and freeze great.

Ingredients

  • Sauce
  • 3 tbsp. butter
  • 3 tbsp. flour
  • 3 1/4 cups chicken stock
  • 1 4oz can green chilies
  • 1/2 cup salsa verde I like spicy for this
  • 1 1/2 cups sour cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 2 cups shredded Monterrey Jack or Pepper Jack cheese
  • Salt & pepper
  • Filling
  • 1 large vidalia onion chopped small
  • 1-2 Jalapenos chopped small
  • 4 oz can green chilies
  • 1/2 cup salsa verde
  • 4 cups shredded chicken
  • 3/4 cup chicken stock
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt & pepper
  • 3/4 cup heavy cream
  • Assembly
  • 2 cups shredded Monterey Jack or Pepperjack cheese
  • 7-8 large flour tortillas

Steps

  1. In a medium pot, melt butter, add flour and whisk until combined. Slowly pour in chicken stock, whisking while you do.
  2. Add a can (4oz) of chilies, salsa verde, sour cream, paprika and cheese and whisk until smooth.
  3. Taste and add pepper and if necessary add some salt.
  4. Set aside.
  5. Heat 2 TBS grapeseed or olive oil in a medium pot, add onions and jalapeno and saute for 2 minutes.
  6. Add chicken, a small 4 oz can of green chilies, salsa verde, stock, and paprika. Stir to combine.
  7. Add cream, salt, and pepper and cayenne if you choose).
  8. Heat until mixture begins to bubble, set aside.
  9. Add a small amount of sauce to the bottom of your baking dish. (preheat oven to 375 degrees)
  10. Fill each tortilla with a couple of big spoonfuls of the mixture, sprinkle shredded cheese over top and roll up.Place in baking dish and repeat until all mixture is used up.
  11. Spoon sauce over top the enchiladas and bake for 20 minutes.
  12. Remove from oven, sprinkle with cheese and return to the oven until cheese is melted.
  13. Sprinkle with parsley, cilantro and/or scallions and serve.
